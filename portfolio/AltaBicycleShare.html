I spent several formative years doing cartography, graphic design, and web development for bicycle share systems around the country, including Boston, New York, Chicago, Chattanooga, and Columbus. My responsibilities ranged from designing instructional graphics for the field to collaborating with city governments to shape the visual hierarchy of system maps to be displayed at stations. Of personal pride is the ever-growing Hubway system which I helped launch in Boston, Massachusetts, and continues to grow yearly.
